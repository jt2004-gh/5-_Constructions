About

Professional Construction Services Since 2021.

5& Construction is a trusted name in Federal Way, Washington, specializing in managing construction projects from start to finish. With our team of skilled professionals, we transform your vision into reality with precision and excellence.

We hold a Residential Alteration Permit which allows us to oversee all types of construction projects including plumbing, drywall, roofing, sprinkler fitting, tile setting, window replacement, smoke damage restoration, complete home remodeling and many more!

Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, transparent communication, and customer satisfaction sets us apart in the industry. We believe in building not just structures, but lasting relationships with our clients.
